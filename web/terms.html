Description of the Game

Word Puzzle Battle is a free, browser-based, competitive word-building game. Two players take turns placing letters on a shared 10×10 grid. Valid English words score points. First to 20 points wins.

The Game includes single-player mode against AI, real-time multiplayer via Firebase, player statistics tracking, optional Google account sign-in, and advertisements served by Google AdSense and AdMob.
