01 —

Who We Are

Word Puzzle Battle ("the Game", "we", "us", "our") is a free browser-based word game developed and operated by Prarambha Bashyal, based in Nepal.

The Game is accessible at wordpuzzlebattle.web.app and wordpuzzlebattle.firebaseapp.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you play. By playing, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

02 —

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Your display name (if you create a profile)
  • Your Google account name and profile photo (if you sign in with Google)
  • Your Google account email address (used only for account identification)

Information collected automatically:

  • Game statistics — games played, games won, words found, total points
  • Device type and browser type (for compatibility)
  • General geographic region (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Pages visited within the Game and time spent
  • IP address (collected by Google's infrastructure, not stored by us directly)

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Your precise GPS location
  • Your contacts or phone book
  • Your camera or microphone
  • Your financial or payment information

03 —

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Run the game and provide multiplayer functionality
  • Save and display your game statistics across devices
  • Identify your account when you sign in with Google
  • Show relevant advertisements via Google AdSense and AdMob
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent cheating and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

04 —

Google AdSense and Advertising

Word Puzzle Battle uses Google AdSense and Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services are provided by Google LLC.

Google uses cookies and similar technologies to show you ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads or aboutads.info.

What Google AdSense collects via our site: Cookie identifiers, IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time and date of visit. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

05 —

Firebase and Google Services

The Game uses the following Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication — handles Google Sign-In. Your Google account ID is stored to identify your account.
  • Firebase Realtime Database — stores your game statistics and multiplayer room data. Data is stored on Google's servers in Europe (europe-west1).
  • Firebase Hosting — serves the game files. Standard web server logs (IP, browser, pages) are collected by Google.

Firebase's data practices are governed by Google's Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

06 —

Cookies and Local Storage

Local Storage (in your browser):

  • wpb-visual-theme — your chosen colour theme
  • wpb-settings — your audio and difficulty preferences
  • wpb-tutorial-completed — whether you have seen the tutorial
  • wpb-profile — local guest profile data (if not signed in with Google)

Cookies set by Google AdSense/AdMob: Used for ad personalisation and frequency capping. Can be managed at google.com/settings/ads.

07 —

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only with Google Firebase (to operate the game) and Google AdSense/AdMob (to display advertisements), or when required by law.

Other players can see your display name and score during multiplayer sessions. No other information is shared with other players.

08 —

Data Retention

  • Account data — kept until you delete your account or request deletion
  • Game statistics — kept until account deletion
  • Multiplayer room data — automatically deleted after 40 minutes of inactivity
  • Local storage — stays in your browser until you clear it

09 —

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. To exercise these rights, use the Settings page in the game or contact us as described in Section 13. We will respond within 30 days.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via google.com/settings/ads.

10 —

Children's Privacy

Word Puzzle Battle is intended for players aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

11 —

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Firebase Security Rules restrict database access to authorised users only. We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Google. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

12 —

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and may provide notice within the Game for significant changes.
